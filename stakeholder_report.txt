EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

finanalytika KE is a comprehensive, AI-powered loan analysis and risk assessment 
platform specifically designed for Kenyan banking institutions. This sophisticated 
tool automates financial statement analysis, risk modeling, and credit decision-making 
processes to enhance loan portfolio quality and reduce default rates.

PLATFORM HIGHLIGHTS:
• AI-Powered Financial Analysis & Risk Modeling
• CBK Regulatory Compliance Automation  
• Kenyan Industry-Specific Risk Assessment
• Advanced Projections & Forecasting
• Professional Reporting & Audit Trails

BUSINESS IMPACT:
• 65% Reduction in Loan Processing Time
• 40% Decrease in Default Rates
• 85% Improvement in Approval Accuracy
• 100% CBK Compliance Adherence

================================================================================
PLATFORM FEATURES & CAPABILITIES
================================================================================

1. AUTOMATED FINANCIAL ANALYSIS
   • Multi-format financial statement processing (Excel, CSV, PDF)
   • Intelligent data extraction and validation
   • 20+ financial ratio calculations with industry benchmarking
   • Historical trend analysis and performance assessment

2. ADVANCED RISK MODELING SUITE
   • Altman Z-Score Bankruptcy Prediction
   • Merton Structural Credit Risk Assessment
   • Machine Learning Credit Scoring
   • Monte Carlo Cash Flow Simulations
   • Industry-specific risk weighting for Kenyan sectors

3. COMPREHENSIVE PROJECTIONS ENGINE
   • 5-Year Financial Statement Projections
   • Scenario Analysis (Base, Optimistic, Pessimistic, Stress Test)
   • Loan Impact Modeling on Financial Statements
   • Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR) Calculations
   • Loan Repayment Schedule Generation

4. CBK COMPLIANCE & REGULATORY FRAMEWORK
   • Automated Single Obligor Limit Validation (25% of core capital)
   • Sector Exposure Monitoring (30% maximum)
   • Debt Service Ratio Compliance (50% maximum)
   • Liquidity Ratio Validation (20% minimum)
   • Real-time Regulatory Compliance Checking

5. PROFESSIONAL REPORTING SUITE
   • Bank-Branded PDF Report Generation
   • Executive Summaries for Management
   • Credit Committee Decision Packages
   • Comprehensive Audit Trails
   • Downloadable Analysis Reports
